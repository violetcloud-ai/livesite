About the Company

Greater Vancouver, BC

Established in 2020, Violet Cloud is dedicated to creating computer programs for automating structural engineering; integrating structural analysis, structural design, Building Information Modelling (BIM), and automation of drawing sheet generation.

Our goal is to ultimately provide time and cost savings for the user, where high-level geometric inputs (such as the building size) are translated into polished engineered structural models with details in just a few clicks.

This is a streamlined process to combine the engineering, modelling, detailing and drafting into one integrated and simplified workflow.

Our Professional Engineer (P.Eng) has over 15 years of civil and structural engineering experience in consulting, contracting, 3D modelling and computer programming.
